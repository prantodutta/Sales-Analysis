# Sales-Analysis
# 📊 Fashion Retail Sales & Financial Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end Business Intelligence dashboard built in Power BI using retail sales and financial data to generate actionable business insights. This project analyzes sales performance, revenue trends, product demand, store-wise performance, and financial growth metrics through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 🔹 Business Problem

The organization was facing challenges in tracking and analyzing sales and financial performance across multiple stores and product categories due to fragmented and unstructured data from the Cloud POS system.

This limited the ability to make fast, data-driven decisions regarding inventory distribution, marketing strategy, and product performance optimization.

A centralized Power BI dashboard was required to convert raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

---

## 🔹 Project Goal
To transform raw retail sales data into meaningful insights and interactive dashboards that help businesses monitor performance, optimize operations, and support strategic decision-making.


---

## 🔹 Key Features
- Sales Performance Analysis  
- Financial KPI Monitoring  
- YoY, QoQ & MoM Growth Analysis  
- Product Category & Size Insights  
- Store-wise Sales Comparison  
- Revenue & Quantity Tracking  
- Interactive Power BI Visualizations  
- Business Decision Support Dashboard  

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Excel  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Business Intelligence & Data Analytics  

---

## 🔹 Business Insights Generated
- Identified top-selling product categories and stores  
- Analyzed customer purchasing trends  
- Tracked financial growth over multiple years  
- Evaluated monthly and quarterly sales performance  
- Improved data-driven decision-making using KPI dashboards  

---

## 🔹 Dataset Overview
The dataset contains retail fashion sales transactions including:
- Product Categories  
- Store Information  
- Sales Quantity  
- Revenue (NET AMT)  
- Product Size & Color  
- Time-based Sales Data  

---

## 🔹 Data Source
This project is based on retail sales and financial transaction data extracted from a cloud-based Point of Sale (Cloud POS) system. The dataset was anonymized and processed for analysis, reporting, and dashboard development using Power BI and Excel.

---

## 🔹 Business Impact

Based on the insights generated from this dashboard, the company was able to make more informed distribution decisions and improve its marketing policies. The data-driven approach helped identify high-performing products, stores, and customer segments, which contributed to better inventory planning and targeted marketing strategies.

As a result, the company experienced an increase in overall sales performance and improved operational efficiency through more effective decision-making.
